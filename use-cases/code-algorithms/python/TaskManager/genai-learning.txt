Generative AI Course – What I Learned

During the Generative AI course, I learned the fundamentals of how artificial intelligence can generate content such as text and code.

Key things I learned include:

1. Introduction to Generative AI
I learned that Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that creates new content instead of only analyzing data. Examples include AI that can generate text, images, music, and code.

2. How AI Models Work
I gained an understanding of how large language models are trained using large datasets to recognize patterns and generate human-like responses.

3. Prompt Engineering
I learned how to write clear and specific prompts to get better responses from AI systems. Good prompts help AI produce more accurate and useful outputs.

4. Practical Applications
The course showed how Generative AI can be used in many fields such as software development, content creation, education, and automation.

5. Responsible Use of AI
I also learned the importance of using AI responsibly, including understanding ethical considerations, bias, and data privacy.

Conclusion
This course helped me understand how Generative AI works and how it can be used as a tool to improve productivity and creativity. I am excited to continue learning more about AI and applying these skills in my future projects.
